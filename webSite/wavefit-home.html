Home Exercises

Get fit anywhere - no equipment needed

Why Home Workouts?

Perfect for busy schedules and limited budgets. Get great results at home!

Benefits

  • No gym membership needed
  • Save time on travel
  • Exercise anytime
  • Privacy and comfort
  • Can do anywhere

Full Body Workout (No Equipment)

30 minutes - Beginner to Intermediate

Exercise Sets Reps Rest
Jumping Jacks (warm-up) 1 50 30 sec
Push-ups 3 10-15 60 sec
Squats 3 15-20 60 sec
Plank 3 30-60 sec 60 sec
Lunges 3 12 each leg 60 sec
Mountain Climbers 3 20 total 45 sec
Pike Push-ups 3 8-12 60 sec
Glute Bridges 3 15-20 45 sec
Bicycle Crunches 3 20 total 45 sec
Burpees 2 10 90 sec

7-Day Home Workout Plan

Monday - Upper Body

Push-ups (4x12), Pike Push-ups (3x10), Tricep Dips (3x12), Plank (3x45sec)

Tuesday - Lower Body

Squats (4x20), Lunges (3x15 each), Jump Squats (3x10), Wall Sit (3x45sec)

Wednesday - Core & Cardio

Plank (3x60sec), Mountain Climbers (4x20), Burpees (3x10), High Knees (3x30sec)

Thursday - Full Body

Circuit (3 rounds): Push-ups x10, Squats x15, Plank x30sec, Lunges x10 each, Burpees x8

Friday - HIIT

30sec work / 15sec rest: Jumping Jacks, Burpees, High Knees, Mountain Climbers (Repeat 4x)

Saturday - Active Recovery

Yoga, stretching, or light walking

Sunday - Rest

Complete rest day

Make Exercises Easier or Harder

Easier Versions

Exercise Easier Version

Harder Versions

Exercise Harder Version

Quick 10-Minute Workouts

Morning Energy Boost

45 seconds each, 15 seconds rest (repeat 2x):

  • Jumping Jacks
  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • High Knees
  • Plank

Abs Blast

30 seconds each, no rest (repeat 2x):

  • Crunches
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Plank
  • Russian Twists
  • Leg Raises

Lower Body Burner

12 reps each (repeat 2-3x):

  • Squats
  • Lunges (each leg)
  • Jump Squats
  • Glute Bridges
  • Wall Sit (30 sec)

Success Tips

  • Create Space: Dedicate an area for workouts
  • Set Schedule: Same time each day
  • Wear Workout Clothes: Gets you in the mood
  • Use Timer: Keep workouts structured
  • Stay Hydrated: Keep water nearby
  • Follow Plan: Don't randomly pick exercises
  • Track Progress: Write down your workouts
  • Warm Up: Always take 5 minutes to warm up
