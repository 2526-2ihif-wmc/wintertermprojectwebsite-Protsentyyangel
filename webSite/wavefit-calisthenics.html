Calisthenics Mastery

Learn advanced bodyweight skills

Advanced

Planche

The planche is one of the most impressive calisthenics skills where you hold your body parallel to the ground with only your hands.

How to Achieve

  1. Master the frog stand - lean forward with knees on elbows
  2. Practice planche leans against a wall
  3. Work on tuck planche - body in ball position
  4. Progress to straddle planche - legs spread apart
  5. Finally achieve full planche - legs together

Training Schedule

Week Exercise Sets Hold Time
1-4 Frog Stand 5 20-30 seconds
5-12 Tuck Planche 5 10-15 seconds
13-24 Advanced Tuck 4 10-15 seconds
25-40 Straddle Planche 4 5-10 seconds
Intermediate

Handstand

Balance upside down on your hands.

Steps to Learn

  1. Build wrist strength with plank holds
  2. Practice wall handstands
  3. Hold wall handstands for 60 seconds
  4. Practice kick-ups away from wall
  5. Work on balance by holding a few seconds
  6. Increase time gradually
Advanced

Muscle-Up

Combine a pull-up with a dip to get above the bar.

Prerequisites

  • 15+ pull-ups
  • 20+ dips
  • Explosive pulling power

Progression

Level Exercise Reps
1 High Pull-Ups 3 x 8
2 Jumping Muscle-Ups 3 x 5
3 Negative Muscle-Ups 3 x 5
4 Full Muscle-Up 3 x 3
Advanced

Front Lever

Hold your body horizontal while hanging from a bar.

Progression Steps

  1. Tuck front lever - hold 10-15 seconds
  2. Advanced tuck - one leg extended - hold 8-12 seconds
  3. Straddle front lever - legs spread - hold 5-10 seconds
  4. One leg front lever - hold 5-8 seconds
  5. Full front lever - hold 5+ seconds

Training Frequency

Skill Level Days per Week Rest Between Sessions
Beginner 3-4 48 hours
Intermediate 4-5 36-48 hours
Advanced 5-6 24-36 hours
