Building Muscle (Hypertrophy)

Hypertrophy training focuses on muscle growth through mechanical tension, muscle damage, and metabolic stress. This is ideal for aesthetic goals and building size.

Key Hypertrophy Principles

üéØ Rep Range

8-12 reps per set is optimal for muscle growth

‚è±Ô∏è Time Under Tension

40-60 seconds per set maximizes muscle growth

üìä Volume

üîÑ Frequency

Rep Ranges for Different Goals

Different rep ranges serve different purposes. Understanding these helps you train specifically for your goals.

Strength 1-5 reps 3-6 sets 3-5 min Maximum force production
Muscle Growth 6-12 reps 3-5 sets 1-2 min Optimal hypertrophy stimulus
Endurance 12-20+ reps 2-4 sets 30-60 sec Muscular endurance, Fatigue

Safety Tips & Gym Etiquette

Critical Safety Rules

1

Always Warm Up: 5-10 minutes cardio and dynamic stretching before lifting

2

Use a Spotter: Have someone watch you for heavy compound lifts

3

Start Light: Learn proper form before adding significant weight

4

Listen to Pain: Stop immediately if you feel sharp or unusual pain

5

Stay Hydrated: Drink water throughout your workout session

6

Cool Down & Stretch: 5-10 minutes of static stretching post-workout

Gym Etiquette

  • Wipe Equipment: Clean machines and benches after use
  • Re-rack Weights: Always return weights to their proper place
  • Share Equipment: Allow others to use equipment between sets
  • No Phone Calls: Keep conversations quiet and brief
  • Respect Space: Don't stand too close or interrupt workouts
  • Don't Drop Weights: Lower weights controlled (except for deadlifts)
